# MiniKhedma

## Overview

MiniKhedma is an AI-powered platform that connects students with families and local businesses that need short-term help. Many students already have practical skills such as tutoring, cooking, childcare, or basic tech repair, but often lack a simple way to turn those abilities into income. At the same time, many households and small businesses need affordable and flexible assistance for everyday tasks. SkillUp bridges this gap by creating a local marketplace where students can offer micro-services and clients can quickly find help nearby.

The platform uses AI to make the onboarding process easier and more accessible. Instead of asking students to fill out complicated forms, an AI agent interacts with them through a natural conversation. During this process, the system identifies the student’s skills, generates a clear profile, and helps present their services in a way that clients can easily understand. This lowers the barrier to entry and allows students to join the platform even if they have never used freelance or gig platforms before.

By combining AI-driven skill discovery with a trusted local marketplace, SkillUp aims to unlock the hidden potential of student skills while helping communities access reliable and affordable help.
